Bihar Purvanchal Samaj

Bihar Purvanchal Samaj Gandhinagar

Bihar Purvanchal Samaj is a 12A registered and 80G approved NGO based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. We are dedicated to preserving Bihar and Purvanchal's rich cultural heritage and supporting the welfare of the Bihari community living across Gujarat. All donations to our organisation are eligible for income tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Our Mission

Bihar Purvanchal Samaj was founded to unite Bihari and Purvanchal families living in Gujarat, preserve our cultural identity, and give back to the community through social welfare activities. We work towards cultural development, community bonding, and charitable service for families from Bihar and Uttar Pradesh residing in Gandhinagar and Vadodara.

Cultural Festivals We Celebrate

We bring the spirit of Bihar and Purvanchal to Gujarat through grand celebrations of Chhath Puja in Gandhinagar, Makar Sankranti, Holi Milan Samaroh, Ram Navami, Janmashtami, and Navratri. These events unite Bihari and Purvanchal families across Gandhinagar, Vadodara, and Ahmedabad, keeping our Bhojpuri and Maithili cultural roots alive far from home.

Social & Community Welfare

In addition to cultural activities, Bihar Purvanchal Samaj is committed to the overall welfare of our community. We regularly organise social awareness programs, community gatherings, and charitable initiatives that support underprivileged families, encourage education, and promote health awareness among the Bihar and Purvanchal community in Gujarat.

Bihar & Purvanchal Community in Gujarat

As a leading Bihar samaj in Gujarat, we serve the Bihari diaspora across Gandhinagar, Vadodara, Ahmedabad, and surrounding cities. Bihar Purvanchal Samaj is a registered Bihar association and Purvanchal sabha — the trusted community organisation for North Indian families in Gujarat. We help newcomers from Bihar and Uttar Pradesh settle in, connect, and find a second home.

Donate with 80G Tax Benefit

Bihar Purvanchal Samaj holds an 80G approval certificate issued by the Income Tax Department of India. Individuals and companies can donate to our NGO and claim income tax deduction on the donated amount. We welcome individual donations as well as CSR contributions from organisations seeking to support Bihar's cultural heritage and community welfare in Gujarat.
